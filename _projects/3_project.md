### Matter and model building 

The **proton** carries a positive electric charge, and a specific chemical element is identified by the number of protons in its nucleus. This number is called the **atomic number** of the element.

In addition to atomic number, a second number - **mass number** defined as the number of protons plus neutrons in a nucleus - characterizes atoms.
